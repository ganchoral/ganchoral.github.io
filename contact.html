How to Join The Gananoque Choral Society

We meet on Tuesday nights

at the Christ Church Anglican Parish Hall

on Church St. in Gananoque

from 7:15 PM to 9:00 PM

during fall, winter and spring.

Unlike real jobs, we get summers off.

Our purpose is

to bring the joy of singing to others

and just generally enjoy singing ourselves

To find out more,

call Dave Grant at 613-540-2248

.
